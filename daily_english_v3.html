The Perfect Morning: How People Start Their Day
From coffee lovers to early risers, everyone has a morning ritual. Let's explore what a typical morning looks like in English.
A2 LEVEL · 3-MINUTE READ · TOPIC: DAILY LIFE

Every morning, millions of people around the world wake up and begin their day in different ways. Some people set an alarm for 6 a.m. Others prefer to sleep in until 8 or 9.

After waking up, most people brush their teeth and wash their face. Then comes breakfast. Many people say it is the most important meal of the day.

In Taiwan, many people eat at a breakfast shop. In the UK, people often eat toast. In the USA, cereal or pancakes are popular.

After breakfast, people commute to work or school. A good morning ritual helps you feel energized all day.
